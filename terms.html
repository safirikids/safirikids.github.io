Terms

Terms, briefly.

Last updated: April 2026

Use

Safirikids is licensed for use by schools, their parents, and their drivers. Authorised users may use the app, the API, and any data exported about their own children, route or vehicle.

Payment

Per-child / per-term, billed monthly to the school. Invoices are due 14 days from issue. Late payment doesn't suspend the parent app — we don't punish parents for an admin invoice.

What we promise

99.5% uptime during route hours. Encrypted backups. Annual security audit. We will tell you within 72 hours of discovering anything resembling a breach.

What we don't promise

Bus locations are estimates from GPS — we cannot guarantee accuracy below 10 metres in dense urban areas. We do not promise the bus will be on time; that's between you and the driver.

Cancellation

Cancel any time, in writing or in the dashboard. We pro-rate the running term and refund any unused portion. We export your data in a format you can read.

Disputes

Governed by the laws of Kenya. We try, in good faith, to resolve disputes by talking first.
