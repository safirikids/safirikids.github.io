Features

Everything a calm morning needs.

We built Safirikids for drivers who don't want to look at their phone, parents who don't want to look at their phone, and schools who don't want to look at WhatsApp.

For parents

Section 01 / 03
01

Live bus on a paper-style map

GPS refreshed every twelve seconds. The map is calm and legible — designed for a quick glance at the kettle, not a doomscroll.

02

Calm notifications

‘Two stops away.’ ‘At school.’ ‘Home safe.’ That's it. No marketing pings, no streaks, no badges to clear.

03

Verified drop-off

Every alighting is signed: photo at the gate, guard's tap, GPS pin. Long-pressed in chat? Already verified.

04

School-mediated driver line

Tap to call the driver — but only on rotation defined by the school, only during route hours.

For drivers

Section 02 / 03
01

Today's manifest

Names, stops, photos. One scrolling list. Greens and yellows do the rest. Works in a tunnel.

02

Hands-free voice notes

Press to talk, release to send. Goes straight to the school's transport coordinator.

03

Offline-first

Boarding, alighting and notes queue when there's no signal. Sync resumes silently when you're back.

04

Earnings & payouts

See route fees, kilometre logs and weekly payouts in one place. No spreadsheet, no chasing.

For schools

Section 03 / 03
01

Routes & vehicles

Draw routes on a map. Assign drivers, assistants, and students. Roll over a term in minutes.

02

Per-term fees & invoices

Set transport fees per route. Invoices auto-generate, integrate with the school's billing.

03

Audit trail

Every boarding, alighting, photo, message — timestamped and signed. Export per term, per route, per child.

04

Pickup codes & guardian rotation

Fresh 4-digit code per pickup. Guardians and emergency contacts you can rotate per week.
